Core Features

Everything you need to monitor student progress

From uploading a hackathon certificate to publishing a research paper — every achievement is tracked, verified, and scored.

📤

Achievement Upload

Students upload certificates, papers, patents and hackathon results with supporting documents in a single, simple form.

⚡

Automatic Scoring

Predefined point rules assign scores instantly on approval. The system calculates totals across all categories automatically.

✅

Faculty Verification

Faculty review submitted achievements, approve or reject entries, and leave remarks. Verified records are counted in final scores.

📊

Dashboards

Admins and faculty get a full overview of class performance, top achievers, and achievement distribution across semesters.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Students, faculty, and administrators each have tailored dashboards and permissions. Data is always shown to the right person.

🏆

Live Leaderboard

Students can see their rank among peers. Faculty and admins see leaderboards filtered by department or semester.
